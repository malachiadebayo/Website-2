CSS stands for cascading style sheets.
    Can be applied in 3 ways
        1. Inline <p style=" color:red;"> text </p>
        2. Embedded
        <head>
            <style> 
                p {color:red}
            </style>
         </head>   
        3. Linked 
            p {color:red;}  

Images for websites can be JPG (photographic images with a lot of color), GIF or PNG(similar to JPG) - these are known as raster images.
Can also be an SVG, which is made up of XML code. This cannot be manipulated in photoshop.
